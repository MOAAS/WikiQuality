'''Frederik Albert "Frits" Palm''' (born 1947 - died 2011) was a Dutch economist, civil servant and former politician for the [[Pim Fortuyn List]] in the [[House of Representatives (Netherlands)|House of Representatives]] from 2002 to 2003.

Before he was elected to parliament, Palm was the director of the Cabinet of Antillean Affairs. In parliament he focused on Foreign Affairs, European Affairs, Defense and Kingdom Relations.
