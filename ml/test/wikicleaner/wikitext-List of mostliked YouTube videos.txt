This '''list of most-liked YouTube videos''' contains the top 30 videos with the most likes of all time, taken directly from the video page. [[YouTube]] implemented a [[Like button|like and dislike button]] on these pages in March 2010, part of a major redesign of the site. This served as a replacement for their [[Star (classification)|five-star]] rating system;<ref>{{cite web|last=Lowensohn|first=Josh|date=March 31, 2010|url=http://www.cnet.com/news/youtubes-big-redesign-goes-live-to-everyone/|title=YouTube's big redesign goes live to everyone|work=[[CNET]]|publisher=[[CBS Interactive]]|access-date=May 9, 2016|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160512000421/http://www.cnet.com/news/youtubes-big-redesign-goes-live-to-everyone/|archive-date=May 12, 2016|df=mdy-all}}</ref> YouTube's designers found the previous system ineffective because the options to rate a video between two and four stars were rarely selected.<ref>{{cite web|last=Siegler|first=M. G.|url=https://techcrunch.com/2009/09/22/youtube-comes-to-a-5-star-realization-its-ratings-are-useless/|title=YouTube Comes To a 5-Star Realization: Its Ratings Are Useless|work=[[TechCrunch]]|publisher=[[AOL|AOL Inc.]]|date=September 22, 2009|access-date=May 9, 2016|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170603231801/https://techcrunch.com/2009/09/22/youtube-comes-to-a-5-star-realization-its-ratings-are-useless/|archive-date=June 3, 2017|df=mdy-all}}</ref>

The music video for [[LMFAO]]'s song "[[Party Rock Anthem#Music video|Party Rock Anthem]]" stood as the most-liked video on YouTube in 2012, with 1.56 million likes, until the video for [[Psy]]'s "[[Gangnam Style (music video)|Gangnam Style]]" surpassed it in September that year with more than 1.57 million likes. Following this accomplishment, "Gangnam Style" entered the ''[[Guinness World Records]]'' book as the most-liked video on YouTube and on the Internet as of 2012. Psy's video remained the most-liked on YouTube for nearly four years until August 27, 2016, when [[Wiz Khalifa]]'s "[[See You Again#Music video|See You Again]]" featuring [[Charlie Puth]] surpassed it with 11.21 million likes. Less than a year later, on July 25, 2017, [[Luis Fonsi]]'s "[[Despacito#Music video|Despacito]]" featuring [[Daddy Yankee]] claimed the top spot with 16.01 million likes.
